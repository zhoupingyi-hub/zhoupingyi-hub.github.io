About Me

I am a researcher, engineer, and entrepreneur working at the intersection of Foundation Models, Spatial Intelligence, and Physical AI.

Today, as the Co-Founder and Chief Scientist of BitInf, I am leading the development of native vectorized 3D foundation models for the Physical AI era. Our goal is to enable AI systems not only to understand language, but also to understand, generate, reason about, and operate within structured three-dimensional physical environments.

Research Vision

From Language Intelligence to Spatial Intelligence

The past decade of AI has been dominated by language models. We believe the next decade will be defined by models capable of understanding and interacting with the physical world. At BitInf, we are building a new generation of Spatial Foundation Models that bridge language, geometry, engineering knowledge, and physical reasoning.

Stage 1

Spatial Generation

Generate structured 3D representations from multimodal inputs

Text-to-3D Image-to-3D Point-to-3D 3D-Editor
Stage 2

Physical Understanding

Enable AI systems to understand geometry, topology, constraints, and physical properties of objects and environments

Stage 3

Spatial Reasoning

Develop foundation models capable of reasoning over structured 3D worlds and engineering systems

Stage 4

Embodied Execution

Connect spatial intelligence with robotics, manufacturing systems, and embodied agents capable of interacting with the physical world
